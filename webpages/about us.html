About Us

Company Profile PC Technowledge Center P. Ltd. was incorporated as a Private Limited Company, in Mumbai, India in the year 1980, and its Software Marketing division was established in the financial year 1987-88. Software Development Division was formed in the year 1999, with a very small and core team of self-motivated technocrats with the dream of developing and implementing socially relevant software projects at affordable costs, yet having immense value to the community at large. The dream was successful when the first University/Technical Board Result Processing Application successfully completed the Results of its first customer, covering 20,000 plus students’ results. Over the years, organization grew, new customers were acquired, new frontiers conquered. PC Center now operates out of its Head Office and its development center at Mumbai, very conveniently and strategically located for the benefit of customers and employees.
